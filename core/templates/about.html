Twitter Next

Twitter is an online news and social networking site where people communicate in short messages called tweets. Tweeting is posting short messages for anyone who follows you on Twitter, with the hope that your messages are useful and interesting to someone in your audience.
TwitterNext is a clone of Twitter’s functionality with a futuristic look. User is able to sign in, create tweets that support hash tagging, delete tweet, and have individual profile page. The main framework is built on Django.
Have fun Tweeting!
